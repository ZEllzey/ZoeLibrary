Welcome to my library! This webpage is a live catalog of the books in my library and some basic info about them. I hope to provide insite as to the books I have chosen to own in my personal collection and maybe you will find a few books you will enjoy as I have.

Below is access to my digitized library. Inside you will find a list of books displaying information including:

  • Title
  • Genre
  • Author
  • Page count
  • and "To be read" Status

You will soon see I have the book divided by "completed" and "To be Read", to display the books I have chosen to complete already and those whose contents I am yet to discover.
